Welcome! The Work Order Tracking System (WOTS) was originally created for CenterPoint Energy (CNP) GIS employees to track the work order flow, but now it is widely used by GIS, Work Order Management (WOM), Operation, Engineering, Compliance, and Leadership to track Gas, Electric, Major Underground and Landbase requests, work orders and data corrections submitted for digitizing into GIS. Requests are primarily submitted by internal company employees as work orders to digitize. CNP and Contract personnel use the application for data entry, QC, problem resolution, work assignment, progress tracking and reporting.

To learn how to use the site, download the WOTS User Guide.

Supported internet browsers for WOTS are Microsoft Edge and Google Chrome. Do not use Internet Explorer to access this site.
